Overall Idea

RIT needs to be moved out of Rochester, and needs to be rebuilt. It's up to you to create bricks to rebuild the Institute back to it's glory!

Hire students to make bricks for you, and pay them with education, the more building built with your patented brick color makes them work harder

Support education and college workers by building the institute that you would like to go to, out of nothing but bricks!

Mechanics

  • Create bricks yourself
  • Make students make bricks for you
  • Conduct research on bricks
  • Prevent the student government from destroying your brick creation
  • Create all the RITs that you want!
  • And Much More!
