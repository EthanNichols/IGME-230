Concept

RIT needs bricks to rebuild, after being told to move the location of RIT

Start from nothing and accumulate upgrades to increase the amount of bricks you can make exponentially

Game Genre

RIT's Brick Factory is an idle progression game

You start from nothing, and slowly accumulate upgrades to exponentially create bricks

Platforms

RIT's Brick Factory will be availible on any browser and accessible by computers from the very beginning

In the future this could be ported to Android and IOS as a downloadable app.

Story

RIT needs to be moved out of Rochester, and needs to be rebuilt. It's up to you to create bricks to rebuild the Institute back to it's glory!

Hire students to make bricks for you, and pay them with education, the more building built with your patented brick color makes them work harder

Support education and college workers by building the institute that you would like to go to, out of nothing but bricks!

Esthetics

The majority of the game will have simplistic art since the graphics don't need to be complicated.

The style will be with pixel art, since it's a simplistic art form, and can be made easily

Mechanics

  • Clicking to create bricks manually
  • Upgrades to automatically create bricks
  • Recreate mechanics, where you can create more RITs after you finish one RIT

Controls

  • Mouse clicking (Computer only)
  • Finger tapping (touch screen devices / mobile)

Teaching players / learning curve

There isn't much to teach players, and the overall mechanics of the game are simplistic, since there isn't any way to win or lose

The one thing the player can learn is what is the most efficient route to buying upgrades to increase their brick creation exponentially faster.
